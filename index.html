About Me

  • I'm working as a Postdoctoral Researcher in Washington University in St. Louis under the supervision of Prof. S. N. Lahiri.
  • Prior to that, I worked as a Visiting Scientist at the Indian Statistical Institute Kolkata for two months.
  • I completed my Ph.D. in Statistics from IIT Tirupati, focusing on advancements in Space-Time Modeling.
  • My research interests include Spatial Statistics and Extreme Value Theory.
  • My research has studied the properties of resampling methods, neural networks, and LASSO in these setups.
